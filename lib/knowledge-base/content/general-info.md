# SwiftCourse Platform Guide

## Getting Started

Welcome to SwiftCourse! Here's how to navigate and make the most of the platform:

### Navigation
- **Dashboard**: Your home page showing overall progress and module overview
- **Sidebar Menu**: Access all modules and sections (collapsible for more space)
- **Module Pages**: Individual learning content with interactive exercises
- **AI Assistant**: Click the "AI Help" button in the top right for instant assistance

### Progress Tracking
- Your progress is automatically saved as you complete sections
- Green checkmarks indicate completed sections
- Progress bars show your completion percentage
- You can reset progress using the "Reset Progress" button in the header

### Interactive Elements

#### Multiple Choice Questions
- Select the answer you think is correct
- Click "Submit Answer" to check your response
- Receive immediate feedback
- Try again if incorrect

#### Matching Games
- Click on a term, then click on its matching definition
- Correct matches turn green and stay locked
- Incorrect matches turn red briefly, then reset
- Keep trying until all pairs are matched

#### Flashcards
- Click to flip between question and answer
- Use arrow buttons to navigate
- Shuffle for random order
- Great for memorization and review

#### Text Input Exercises
- Type your answer in the provided field
- Submit to check your response
- Receive feedback on your answer

### Using the AI Assistant

The AI Learning Assistant can help you with:
- Questions about course content
- Clarification of concepts
- Additional examples and explanations
- Study tips and strategies
- General questions about the platform

To use the AI Assistant:
1. Click the "AI Help" button in the top right
2. Type your question in the chat
3. Press Enter or click Send
4. Receive an instant response
5. Continue the conversation as needed

### Tips for Success

1. **Complete modules in order**: Content builds progressively
2. **Engage with all exercises**: Active learning improves retention
3. **Use the AI assistant**: Don't hesitate to ask questions
4. **Review regularly**: Revisit completed sections to reinforce learning
5. **Apply concepts**: Try to use what you learn in real situations
6. **Track your progress**: Monitor your advancement through the course
7. **Take breaks**: Learning is more effective with proper rest

### Technical Support

If you encounter any issues:
- Try refreshing the page
- Check your internet connection
- Clear your browser cache
- Use the AI assistant for platform-related questions
- Ensure JavaScript is enabled in your browser

### Privacy and Data

- Your progress is stored locally in your browser
- No personal data is collected without consent
- You can reset your progress at any time
- Chat conversations with AI are not permanently stored

### Best Practices

- **Set a schedule**: Dedicate regular time for learning
- **Minimize distractions**: Find a quiet space to focus
- **Take notes**: Write down key insights
- **Practice actively**: Engage with all interactive elements
- **Ask questions**: Use the AI assistant liberally
- **Review regularly**: Spaced repetition enhances memory
- **Apply learning**: Use concepts in real-world situations

### Keyboard Shortcuts

- **Enter**: Submit answers in text fields
- **Arrow keys**: Navigate slideshows and flashcards
- **Esc**: Close modals and panels
